Brand Palette

The HolaCloud visual language

The following palette defines the visual identity we extend to our product suite. These colors are exposed as CSS custom properties in /css/brand.css for consistent reuse.

Combine deep sapphire foundations with vibrant cerulean and mint highlights to express confidence and innovation. Sand acts as a warm neutral for backgrounds, while slate and ink cover typography.
